# AI Access Governance

AI access governance is the practice of controlling how employees, applications, copilots, agents and API workflows access AI models.

It focuses on the live AI access path rather than only policy documents.

A governed AI access path should answer:

- Who is using AI?
- What business purpose is involved?
- What data is included?
- Which model is used?
- Is the model approved?
- What policy decision is applied?
- What audit evidence is recorded?

## Why it matters

Enterprise AI adoption often grows faster than security and governance controls.

Employees may use public AI tools, approved copilots, internal applications or API-based workflows without consistent visibility.

This creates risks around:

- Sensitive customer data
- Confidential business information
- Unapproved model access
- Incomplete audit evidence
- Unclear accountability
- Unmanaged token usage and cost

SecureAI Gateway is designed to help enterprises make AI access visible, controlled and reviewable.
